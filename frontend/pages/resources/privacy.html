Data Protection

Privacy practices shaped from Lucknow, India

FeedBack Analyzer is architected for regulated workloads. Our privacy controls are maintained by a core governance team in Lucknow with partners in Bengaluru and Frankfurt.

Collection & processing

We ingest only the datasets you upload or sync through the API. Tenant data is logically isolated and encrypted using AES-256 at rest plus TLS 1.3 in transit.

  • Optional regional residency in ap-south-1 for India-focused orgs
  • Metadata stored in PostgreSQL with strict role-based access
  • Logs retained for 30 days unless a customer contract overrides

User rights

Workspace admins can delete datasets, revoke access tokens, and export audit logs at any time. Subject access requests are handled through our Lucknow privacy desk.

  • Deletion jobs cascade through object storage and backups
  • Per-dataset retention timers configurable from 7 to 365 days
  • Support for GDPR, DPDP Act (India), and HIPAA business associate terms

Third parties

We rely on AWS, Snowflake, and trusted email providers solely to deliver the FeedBack Analyzer service. Data is never sold or used to train shared models.

  • Vendors reviewed annually for SOC 2 Type II or ISO 27001
  • Inference models dedicated per tenant; no cross-customer mixing
  • Data processing agreements available for every integration

Questions?

Email privacy@feedbackanalyzer.app to talk directly with our governance lead in Lucknow. We respond with policy references, data maps, and breach-notification language tailored to your review.
